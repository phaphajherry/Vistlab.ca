Indicative Screening

Fast and reliable

VIST developed a fast and reliable screening system as part of a quality control process to identify the level of mold, yeast and aerobic bacteria that may exist in lots of your valuable flower or shake. The system is delivered in pre-packaged kits. All the necessary components are included.

Expert technicians

Our VIST technicians are trained to gather samples in a manner that does not remove any physical flower from licensed facilities. Our samples are then incubated, and data is tabulated to understand plate count levels (Colony Forming Units) Pre and Post VIST technology.

Science + Technology

The VIST Indicative Screening is an important part of the Integrated VIST System. We use science and technology to create repeatable and effective processes.
